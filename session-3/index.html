Scandinavian Society

Welcome to the Scandinavian Societys website! We are a society which celebrates the Scandinavian culture through hosting dinner parties, big nights out and other social activites. Anyone who has an interest in the scandic culture is invited to join! On this site you will find information about our comittee and be able to get an impression of our society.
